# Tic-Tac-Toe
Tic-Tac-Toe is a game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. In this variant of a game realisation you can play against computer or against your real partner.
It's a C++ Console Application. Just launch "Main.cpp"
